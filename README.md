# Tuku: Japanese Language Learning App

## Introduction
Welcome to **Tuku**, an interactive Japanese language learning app designed to help users learn Japanese words and phrases effortlessly. With a focus on engaging learning experiences, Tuku offers a variety of categories enriched with audio pronunciation to enhance your language acquisition journey.

## Features
- **Interactive Categories**: Explore different themes such as greetings, food, travel, and more, making learning contextual and relevant.
- **Audio Pronunciation**: Learn correct pronunciation from native speakers with audio clips for every word and phrase.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Navigate easily through categories and lessons with our intuitive design.
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your learning progress and revisit challenging words and phrases.
- **Engaging Quizzes**: Test your knowledge with quizzes that reinforce your learning.

## Getting Started
1. **Download the App**: The Tuku app is available for iOS and Android.
2. **Create an Account**: Sign up to track your progress.
3. **Start Learning**: Choose a category to begin your journey!

## Technologies Used
- **React Native**: For cross-platform app development.
- **Firebase**: For user management and data storage.
- **Audio APIs**: To provide high-quality audio pronunciation.
